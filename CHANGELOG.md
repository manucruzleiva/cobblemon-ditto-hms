## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-19

### Added
- 15 Pokopia-inspired Ditto HM abilities, each consuming hunger on use.
- Abilities unlock by having the required Pokémon in your Cobblemon party.
- `/dittohms use <ability>` — activate any unlocked ability.
- `/dittohms list` — see all abilities with unlock status and cooldowns.
- Per-ability cooldowns to prevent spam.
- Multiloader: Fabric and NeoForge shipped as one Modrinth version.
