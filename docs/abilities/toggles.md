# Toggle HMs

Toggle HMs are **passive abilities** that stay active until you turn them off.  
Enable/disable them via the **HM Case GUI** (Shift + right-click) or with `/dittohms use <id>`.

---

## Hunger cost

Each enabled toggle **permanently blocks 2 hunger points** from your maximum food bar.  
At your effective cap you still recover health — a slow **Regeneration I** effect is applied automatically.

| Active toggles | Max hunger |
|---|---|
| 0 | 20 / 20 |
| 1 | 18 / 20 |
| 2 | 16 / 20 |
| 3 | 14 / 20 |
| 4 | 12 / 20 |
| 5 | 10 / 20 |
| 6 (all) | 8 / 20 |

## Ability details

### Jump
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

Applies **Jump Boost II** continuously while active.  
Approximately doubles jump height — great for traversal.

---

### Surf
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

While in water, applies **Dolphin's Grace** continuously, significantly increasing swim speed.  
Has no effect on land.

---

### Glide
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

Applies **Slow Falling** continuously.  
You descend very slowly, and fall damage is eliminated.  
Combine with Fly for full aerial control.

---

### Rollout
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

Applies **Speed I** continuously — always a slight movement boost.

---

### Dive
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

Two permanent effects while active:

- **Water Breathing** — never run out of air
- **Natural sinking** — a gentle downward force is applied when underwater, countering the vanilla "bobbing up" behaviour

---

### Flash
**Toggle blocks:** 2 hunger

Grants **Night Vision** permanently (infinite duration, ambient effect — shows `**:**` with no countdown timer).  
Toggle off to remove it instantly.
